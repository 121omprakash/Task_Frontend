PAPER-I

"Chemistry" For Secondary Classes (IX & X).

I. Basic Concepts of Chemistry

  • Importance and scope of Chemistry
  • Laws of chemical combination
  • Dalton's atomic theory, Atomic and Molecular masses
  • Mole concept and molar mass
  • Empirical and molecular formula
  • Stoichiometric calculations

II. Environmental Chemistry

  • Environmental pollution: Air, Water, Soil pollution
  • Chemical reactions in atmosphere: Ozone, Greenhouse effect, Acid rain
  • Strategies to control pollution
  • Green chemistry – principles and applications

III. Hydrocarbons

  • Alkanes: nomenclature, preparation, properties, substitution reactions
  • Alkenes: preparation, properties, addition reactions
  • Alkynes: preparation, properties, reactions
  • Aromatic hydrocarbons (Benzene): structure, resonance, electrophilic substitution reactions

IV. The Organic Chemistry – Basic Principles and Techniques

  • General introduction to organic compounds
  • Classification, nomenclature (IUPAC system)
  • Isomerism – structural and stereoisomerism
  • Methods of purification of organic compounds
  • Basic concepts of reaction mechanism – electron displacement (inductive, resonance, hyperconjugation)

V. The Hydrogen Element

  • Position of hydrogen in periodic table
  • Isotopes of hydrogen
  • Properties of hydrogen, hydrides, water and heavy water
  • Hydrogen peroxide – preparation, properties, uses

VI. The s-Block Element (Alkali & Alkaline Earth Metals)

  • General characteristics, occurrence, electronic configuration
  • Physical and chemical properties
  • Compounds of s-block elements: NaOH, NaCl, Na₂CO₃, NaHCO₃, CaO, Ca(OH)₂, CaCO₃, etc.
  • Biological importance

VII. The p-Block Element

  • General introduction, electronic configuration, oxidation states
  • Boron, Aluminium – properties and uses
  • Important compounds: Borax, Orthoboric acid, Diborane, Aluminium chloride
  • Carbon, Silicon – properties and compounds (SiO₂, silicones, silicates)

IV. Chemical Bonding & Solid State Chemistry

V. Stereochemistry
